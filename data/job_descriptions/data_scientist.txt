Sample Job Description – Data Scientist
Job Title: Data Scientist – Customer Analytics
Location: Bangalore, India (Hybrid)
About the Role:
We are seeking a highly motivated Data Scientist to join our analytics team. The ideal candidate will leverage machine learning, statistical modeling, and business acumen to solve real-world problems such as customer churn prediction, personalization, and recommendation systems.

Key Responsibilities
Collect, clean, and analyze structured and unstructured datasets (SQL, Python).

Develop and deploy ML models (classification, regression, clustering, NLP).

Perform exploratory data analysis (EDA) and generate actionable business insights.

Build predictive models for customer churn, segmentation, and lifetime value (CLV).

Work with cross-functional teams (product, marketing, engineering) to deliver data-driven solutions.

Communicate findings through dashboards, reports, and visualizations (Tableau, Power BI).

Required Skills & Qualifications
Bachelor’s/Master’s in Computer Science, Data Science, Statistics, or related field.

Proficiency in Python (Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn, TensorFlow/PyTorch).

Experience with SQL and data querying.

Strong understanding of machine learning algorithms (Logistic Regression, Random Forest, XGBoost, Clustering, NLP).

Knowledge of cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure) is a plus.

Excellent problem-solving, communication, and presentation skills.
